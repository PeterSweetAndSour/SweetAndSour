Historically, what’s the best month to buy/sell a given stock?
By tracking historic monthly performance, users can ascertain whether seasonal trading patterns exist. When measuring payoff, users must look at average monthly returns. To fully gauge seasonal stock performance, users should also take into account the number of times a stock has increased or decreased for a given month.
