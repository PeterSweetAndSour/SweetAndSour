Instructions/notes:
  1. Columns showing horizontal arrows can be adjusted. Click on and drag to the desired width.
  2. * Month beginning: 1 Jul 04
    ** Week beginning: 20 Jun 04
  3. To change an entry, click on the "U" (update) button at the left of the row.
  4. To save changes, you can click on the "S" (save) button at the left of the row, or the Save button at the bottom of the page.
  5. Use the "Where" line of the update section to specify restrictions on the records returned e.g. "Action equals 400"
  6. You can specify up to three restrictions or "filter sets" on the "Where" line of the update section.
  7. If entering a date to filter on, enter in the conventional format: m/d/yy or m/d/yyyy
  8. Text entries for "Where" line of the update section are NOT case-sensitive.
  9. Text entries for "Where" line of the update section can be "NULL". Note that NULL is not the same as an empty string (leaving the text entry box blank).
  10. Click on a row to highlight the row so you can read across easily; click on it again to remove the highlight. If the page is updated, the highlight will be lost.
  11. To see the ID for the row, hold you mouse over the update/save button at the left of the row.
