Demonstration header

My boss wanted our website to better convey the energy that the Innovation Team hopes to bring to projects and she had previously expressed interest in a header graphic for a city innovation event in Columbia which featured blinking lines at various angles so that was the inspiration. That was a massive .gif file; this is markup and CSS so very lightweight. There was never any thought that we would really adopt this for the site though given that the novelty of animations very quickly turns to annoyance.
