Test intercept survey for the careers home page

You should see a blue box on the side of this page. Click it to get the popup survey but you will get it only once if you click on the "x" or Exit buttons, or submit the survey. If you want to see it again you will need to clear the relevant cookie from your browser:

  1. Hit F12 on your keyboard to bring up Developer Tools
  2. Select Storage (Firefox), or Application (Chrome), on the top menu
  3. Select Cookies
  4. Select https://interceptsurvey.in.dc.gov
  5. Right click on the interceptSurvey cookie and select Delete All
  6. Reload the page
